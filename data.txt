
The American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB) is a prominent private university located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Established in 1994, AIUB has quickly developed a reputation for its high-quality education and modern facilities. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across diverse fields such as engineering, business, arts, and social sciences. AIUB is known for its emphasis on a well-rounded education, integrating academic excellence with practical experience. The campus boasts state-of-the-art laboratories, a comprehensive library, and vibrant student life, encouraging extracurricular engagement alongside rigorous academics. AIUB's commitment to innovation and global standards has made it a significant contributor to the educational landscape in Bangladesh, attracting a diverse student body and fostering a culture of research and development.
